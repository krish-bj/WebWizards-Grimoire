## 🎨 Visual Alchemy (Icons, UI/UX & Design)

### 💎 Hallowed Icons (100% Free / Open Source)
*These artifacts are completely free to use in your projects without hidden costs.*

- [Ionicons](https://ionicons.com/) - 700+ MIT-licensed icons; outline, filled, and sharp styles.
- [Unicons](https://iconscout.com/unicons) - 1,100+ line icons; Figma-ready and no attribution required.
- [Iconizer](https://iconizer.net/) - 1,050+ icons across 4 styles; customize directly in your browser.
- [Iconduck](https://iconduck.com/) - A massive collection of 100% open-source icons and illustrations.
- [FreeIcons](https://freeicons.io/) - Large repository of free SVG/PNG icon sets across many categories.
- [Lucide Icons](https://lucide.dev/) - Beautiful, consistent, and highly customizable icon toolkit.

### 🏛️ Artifact Markets (Clearly Marked Free Tiers)
*High-quality platforms with powerful free-only filters.*

- [Iconfinder (Free-Filtered)](https://www.iconfinder.com/search?price=free) - Huge marketplace with a dedicated "Free" license filter.
- [The Noun Project](https://thenounproject.com/) - Enormous library; filter for CC-licensed or "Free" icons.
- [Freepik (Icon Filter)](https://www.freepik.com/icons) - Tons of icons; use the "Free" filter to find high-quality artifacts.
- [Flaticon (Free Filter)](https://www.flaticon.com/free-icons) - One of the largest databases in the realm; filter by "Free" carefully.

### 🛠️ UI/UX Sorcery Tools
- [Toools.design (Icons)](https://toools.design/free-icons) - A directory of open-source icon libraries to avoid "pseudo-free" traps.
- [Figma Community (Icons)](https://www.figma.com/community/icons) - Search for "open-source" to find 100% free Figma sets.
- [unDraw](https://undraw.co/) - Open-source illustrations for every idea.
- [Storyset](https://storyset.com/) - Customizable free illustrations.
